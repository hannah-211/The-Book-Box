About The Book Box

The Book Box is a site dedicated to getting you started on your reading journey.

Want to start reading but don't know where to start? Have a genre in mind but need a specific book? The Book Box has a book recommendation for you!

Established in 2018, the Book Box is aimed at readers who aren't sure what novel to turn to next, and works on recommendations and reviews of the reading community to keep the list updating.
